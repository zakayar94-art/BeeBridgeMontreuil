BEE BRIDGE INTERACTIVE

ABOUT

Is a narrative road trip in 3D isometric and point and click for PC.

In a postwar world, you will embody a young girl with albinism.

Thanks to her makeshift truck, her goal is to reach her Eldorado. Progress by foot and by truck in a devastated (No) Man's Land.

Through a dying environment populated by memorable characters, be prepared to face your stigmas, and manage your resources to overcome obstacles and survive.
